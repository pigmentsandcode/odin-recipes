Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is time consuming to make, but results in a delicious dish. It's from Italy. This is a very popular recipe.

Ingredients

  • Meat: sweet Italian sausage and ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices: fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
